Exploring Creativity in Minecraft

In this game of endless possibilities, you can unleash your creative power and show it to the world.


What is Minecraft?

Minecraft is a popular sandbox game which allows users to build and create their own works of art. It offers two gamemodes, Survival Mode and Creative mode.


The gamemodes explained

Survival mode allows players to interact with a generated world where players gather resources and survive.

Creative mode lets players have the freedom to do whatever they want. Most of the time, players use this for creating their incredible builds, or test out new ideas.


The Minecraft Creative inventory, where players can pick any block and build right away.


Why choose Minecraft?

Minecraft is a game which is almost limitless in its possibilities.


Some of the things people like to use Minecraft for include:

  • Building structures from real life (e.g. Eiffel Tower)
  • Making contraptions (e.g. Automatic Door)
  • Making art (e.g. Pixel Art)

Taking Minecraft to the next level

One way in which people have gone further in their adventure is by creating Minecraft Servers

Minecraft Servers are online worlds which allow players to play together. Some Minecraft servers such as Hypixel have grown into huge servers with thousands of players daily.

Some Minecraft servers use plugins which adds functionality to the game.
